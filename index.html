My Work

Seedly | Plant Care and eCommerce App

Seedly is a native mobile app for iOS and Android that helps gardening enthusiasts nurture healthier plants through expert guidance, practical tips, and a seamless eCommerce experience. Users can not only access plant care resources but also shop for gardening tools, seeds, and essentials—all within the app.

This project challenged me to design across both Apple and Material Design systems, expanding my expertise beyond iOS. By blending plant care with eCommerce, Seedly showcases my adaptability and commitment to intuitive, high-quality design.

BANQ | Personal Finance App

BANQ is a responsive web app designed to help users save money effortlessly through a seamless, intuitive interface. Focused on trust, simplicity, and personalization, BANQ adapts to individual financial journeys.

I integrated AI to provide tailored recommendations and real-time insights, optimizing saving habits through a user-centric, research-driven approach. By merging cutting-edge AI with intuitive design, BANQ makes finance management accessible, engaging, and effective—empowering users to take control of their savings with ease.

Cookly | Recipe App

Cookly is a responsive web app designed for cooking enthusiasts of all skill levels. Acting as a personal chef assistant, it helps users explore recipes, manage ingredients, and navigate the cooking process with ease.

This project emphasized user testing and direct feedback, allowing me to refine the interface and prioritize user needs. By overcoming designer biases, I ensured a truly user-centric experience, making Cookly an intuitive and practical tool for home cooks and chefs alike.
