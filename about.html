Hi, I'm Leonie Almeida!

I'm a UI/UX Designer based in Houston, TX, dedicated to creating clean, intuitive, and user-centered digital experiences. With expertise in responsive web design and frontend development, I thrive on blending visual aesthetics with psychological insights to craft efficient, engaging interfaces.

My journey into design began with a UI/UX bootcamp, where I discovered my passion for crafting meaningful solutions that balance creativity and functionality. Since then, I've built projects like Seedly, BANQ, and Cookly, applying user research/psychology, wireframing, and prototyping to bring ideas to life.

With a background in Community Health and Psychology, I bring an empathy-driven approach to design, ensuring that every decision is grounded in user needs and behavior. My experience in telemarketing, sales, and healthcare has sharpened my communication, problem-solving, and research skills, all of which play a crucial role in designing intuitive experiences. Additionally, my love for photography enhances my creativity and strengthens my eye for aesthetics, further enriching my work.
