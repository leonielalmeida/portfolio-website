Hi, I'm Leonie Almeida!

I'm a UI/UX Designer based in Houston, TX, creating clean and intuitive digital experiences. With expertise in responsive web design and frontend development, I love bringing ideas to life that balance creativity and functionality.

My journey began with a design bootcamp, where I discovered my passion for crafting meaningful solutions for users. Since then, I've built projects like Seedly, BANQ, and Cookly, combining my skills in UI/UX design and frontend development.

I transitioned from a nursing career to UI/UX design, blending my passion for design with the analytical and problem-solving skills I developed in healthcare. My experience in the medical field gave me a unique perspective on empathy-driven solutions, where attention to detail and collaboration were vital. Additionally, my love for photography enhances my creativity and sharpens my sense of aesthetics, enriching my user-centered design approach.

Outside of work, you can find me experimenting with design ideas, enjoying nature, doing photography or spending time with my two dogs, a Chow Chow named Tiger, and my poodle mix, Max.
