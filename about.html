About Me

My Story

Hi there, it's very nice to meet you! My name is Leonie Almeida and I am a UI/UX Designer based in Houston, Texas.

My passion is curating the most visually pleasing designs and providing the most efficient experience for my users!

I transitioned careers from nursing to UI/UX Design and I couldn't be more honored to pursue this passion of mine for design! My previous professional experience in the medical field has allowed me to have an eye for detail and exceptional teamwork and patient-focused problem solving skills. I also have a passion for photography as well, so enhanced creativity and aesthetic aspects are embebbed in my overall personality. The past year has allowed me to mesh both of my worlds by getting truly integrated with the field of design by studying and mastering all that emcompasses the field of UI!

Some hobbies of mine include traveling, walking/hiking, spending time with my dogs, going to the gym, cooking, and photography.

My goal is to showcase my abilities, while still enhancing my knowledge in the field of design and provide my users with the most delightful, efficient, and unforgettable experience!
